Graduation Requirements

You must fulfill the graduation requirements for each major to be awarded a degree.

  • Credit Requirement: 140 credits (incl. Major, Liberal Arts, Electives)
  • GPA: 2.0 or higher
  • Completion of all required major courses
  • Pass Comprehensive Exam or Capstone Project
