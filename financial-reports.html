Full-time Faculty Introduction

Introducing the core faculty of GTCC Cyber Campus who nurture future capabilities. Professors with academic expertise and industrial experience accompany students on their learning journey.

GTCC faculty collaborate to develop advanced educational content and customized learning coaching, designing classes based on research experience and practical know-how accumulated in their respective fields. We aim for student-centered learning and provide feedback utilizing project-based classes and AI learning analysis.

  • Introduction of AI-based learning analysis and feedback system
  • Execution of global industry-academia cooperation projects and joint research
  • Operation of student mentoring and career coaching programs
