Graduate School Overview

The Graduate School of Theology deeply researches all majors including Biblical Studies, Systematic Theology, Historical Theology, and Practical Theology, producing professional ministers and scholars. We provide theological insights that respond to contemporary issues through interdisciplinary research and global theological networks.

Career Paths

Graduates connect to Senior/Associate Pastors, mission strategists, theological researchers, seminary professors, and NGO/Hospital/Military chaplaincy. We provide a foundation for contributing to international theological journals and post-doctoral research.
