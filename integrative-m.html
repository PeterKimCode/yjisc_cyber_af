Integrative Medicine Major

The Integrative Medicine Major cultivates experts who can design patient-centered treatment plans by fusing Western medicine with complementary and alternative therapies. We present a standard for integrated care by combining evidence-based clinical research with personalized wellness coaching.

Program Vision

The Integrative Medicine Major emphasizes a multidisciplinary team-based approach integrating medicine, nutrition, exercise, and psychotherapy for patient-centered care. We expand the prevention-centered medical paradigm through clinical data analysis and personalized treatment strategies.

Patient-Customized Care

  • Prescriptions based on lifestyle analysis
  • Multidisciplinary team treatment model
  • Long-term tracking management system

Evidence-Based Treatment

  • Latest clinical research seminars
  • Clinical trial design practice
  • Medical big data analysis

Wellness Strategy

  • Nutrition & Exercise Integrated Coaching
  • Stress Management Programs
  • Utilization of Digital Healthcare

Curriculum Structure

The curriculum, consisting of core theory, clinical practice, and research seminars, is designed to allow medical professionals from various backgrounds to collaborate. Students establish integrated treatment plans based on actual patient cases and verify results through capstone projects.

Research and Career

Graduates work in integrative medicine specialized clinics, hospital integrative care centers, wellness companies, and public medical research institutes, conducting joint research with domestic and international academic networks. We support various career paths such as integrative health consultants, clinical researchers, and university lecturing.
