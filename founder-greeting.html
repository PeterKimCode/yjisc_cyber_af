Director's Key Messages

Building a Future Learning Ecosystem

GTCC creates a learning ecosystem combining digital technology and human-centered design to support students in designing and practicing their own learning goals.

  • AI-based learning analysis and customized tutoring
  • Expansion of student-led project classes
  • Operation of personalized learning path design platform

Social Responsibility with the Community

The founder believes that educational institutions must cooperate closely with the local community. GTCC is continuously expanding knowledge sharing and social contribution activities.

  • Operation of joint educational programs with local agencies
  • Expansion of talent donation and mentoring networks
  • Promotion of projects linked to Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)
