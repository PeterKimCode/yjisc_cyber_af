Graduate School Overview

Graduate school is a place to deeply explore knowledge learned in undergraduate studies and develop expertise. Among them, the General Graduate School is an educational institution focused on academic research, chosen by students who wish to advance into academia or research fields.

Curriculum Guide

Master's and Ph.D. programs consist of research seminars, independent studies, and ministry internships, aiming for the integration of academics and ministry. Experience international theological discourse and networks through intensive seminars and cohort learning.
