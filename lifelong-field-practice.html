Field Practicum Program

Field Practicum Guide

GTCC Lifelong Education Center Field Practicum is an essential course to cultivate practical sense and professionalism by connecting major theories to actual work. Based on partnerships with partner organizations, we systematically operate customized learner placement, field mentoring, and performance evaluation.

Duration: 4~12 Weeks Direct: Students completing majors Inquiry: practicum@gtcc.edu
Operation Model Procedure Support

Operation Model

Field practicum consists of three stages: advance preparation, field placement, and performance sharing, and is jointly evaluated by the practicum institution mentor and the school academic supervisor. We manage practicum journals using digital notebooks and help achieve learning goals with real-time feedback.

Preparation

  • Job Understanding Workshop
  • Field Safety & Ethics Training
  • Setting Personal Learning Goals

Field Placement

  • Partner Institution Assignment
  • Mentor-Mentee Weekly Meetings
  • Competency-based Task Performance

Performance Sharing

  • Practicum Report & Portfolio
  • Competency Certification Badge
  • Field Presentation & Feedback

Progress Procedure

  1. Step 1 – Application & Matching: After consulting with major advisor, submit application and get recommendation for practicum institution fitting major/career.
  2. Step 2 – Pre-training: Complete job understanding, etiquette, and safety training and get approval for practicum plan.
  3. Step 3 – Field Performance: Check learning progress with weekly online meetings and share field mentor evaluations in real-time.
  4. Step 4 – Evaluation: Generally evaluate practicum journals, assignments, and presentations to grant credits and competency badges.

Participation Support System

We support practicum preparation costs, insurance subscriptions, and moving expenses to reduce student burden, and provide overseas training opportunities to excellent trainees. We strengthen employment linkage through matching with career consultants after practicum.

Financial Support

  • Practicum Prep Cost max 300k KRW
  • Industrial/Injury Insurance
  • Transportation support for long-distance

Mentoring Program

  • Field Mentor-Student 1:1 Matching
  • Weekly Practicum Coaching Report
  • Best Case Sharing Seminar

Career Linkage

  • Practicum Institution Recruiting Briefing
  • Expert Interview Consulting
  • Employment Recommendation Letter
