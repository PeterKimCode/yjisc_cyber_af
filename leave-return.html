Types of Leave of Absence

Select the leave of absence system that fits your situation.

  • General Leave: Max 2 years for personal reasons
  • Military Leave: Full recognition of military service period
  • Medical Leave: Approved in 1-year units upon submission of medical proof
  • Maternity/Parental Leave: Max 2 years per child
4 Types Leave Types

We operate customized leave of absence systems according to student situations.

Leave of Absence Procedure

Follow these steps to apply for a leave of absence.

  1. Log in to Student Portal and fill out application
  2. Upload proof documents and get department approval
  3. Final approval from Student Affairs
  4. Approval result notification via text and email

Important Notes

Class participation and scholarships are restricted during leave of absence.

Return Procedure

Return applications can be made starting 1 month before the semester begins.

  1. Student Portal > Academic Admin > Apply for Return
  2. Course registration after return approval
  3. Tuition payment and participation in orientation
3 Steps Return Process

Return is confirmed after approval, course registration, and registration completion.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions about leave of absence and return.

Credit requirements for leave?

Students who have completed at least 6 credits in the previous semester can apply.

Schedule after return application?

It takes an average of 3 business days for approval, and results are notified via text.

Scholarships during leave?

Scholarship eligibility is re-evaluated after returning to school.
