Education Framework

Personalized Learning

We diagnose each student's achievement through learning data analysis and provide customized learning content and mentoring.

  • Competency diagnosis report provided every semester
  • Regular learning coaching sessions with tutors
  • AI-based learning recommendation system operation

Project-based Collaboration

We strengthen practical skills and improve teamwork and communication skills through problem-solving projects linked to industry.

  • Capstone Design courses by department
  • Joint projects with global companies
  • Demo Day for sharing achievements
