Information Disclosure

Providing mandatory information disclosed according to the Ministry of Education's university information disclosure system.

Purpose of Disclosure System

Information disclosure is conducted to enhance university transparency and guarantee the public's right to know.

Key indicators such as academic management, financial status, student support, and educational outcomes are regularly disclosed and verified.

  • Disclosure Scope: 14 fields including academics, finance, students, and educational environment
  • Disclosure Cycle: Regular updates twice a year (April, October)
  • Verification System: Monitoring by the Ministry of Education and external agencies

Disclosure Items

Academics and Students

  • Admission quota, number of enrolled students, number of graduates
  • Employment rate by major, transfer/double major status
  • Scholarship payment details and student welfare support

Finance and Facilities

  • Income/expenditure details and audit results
  • Expansion of educational facilities and investment in learning infrastructure
  • Long-term development plan and financial soundness indicators

Performance Indicators

We disclose both qualitative and quantitative indicators reflecting the quality of education and social contribution.

  • Education satisfaction survey results
  • Industry-academia cooperation and research performance
  • Social contribution program participation performance
