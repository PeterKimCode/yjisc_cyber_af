Theology

As a seminary approved by the Presbyterian Church in Korea (Hanyoung General Assembly), we systematically explore the Bible and doctrine to nurture faith leaders who can respond to the requests of the times. Aiming for pastors and theologians with both spirituality and scholarship, we balance biblical interpretation, historical/systematic theology, and practical theology.

Degree: Bachelor (BA) Affiliation: University Duration: 4 Years

Overview

The Theology Department aims to cultivate balanced pastoral ministry capabilities based on biblical language research and understanding of Christian tradition. While exploring the essence of the gospel, we cultivate practical theological sensitivity to interpret and communicate various problems of modern society evangelically.

Career Paths

After graduation, you can advance to various fields such as Presbyterian Church (Hanyoung General Assembly) pastors, missionaries, military/hospital/prison ministers, Christian counselors, NGO activists, etc. We also lay a solid academic foundation for entering domestic and foreign theological graduate schools.

Ministry/Mission

Senior/Assistant Pastor, Mission Org Manager, Community Mission Center Leader

Education/Counseling

Christian Ed Director, Youth Minister, Christian Counseling Expert

Research/Academic

Theology Grad School (Th.M/Ph.D), Christian Research Inst, Publishing & Media Ministry
