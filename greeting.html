A Reliable Partner for Future Education

GTCC is realizing education that does not lose human-centered values even in the flow of digital transformation. The Vice Director's office is carefully preparing everything from learning design to support systems so that students' dreams and careers can become reality.

We help learners design and achieve their own learning goals through data-based bachelor's, master's, and doctoral management and teaching-learning support systems. In addition, we provide experiences connected to the real field through an industry-academia network connecting the region and the world.

GTCC will continue to practice innovation and care as a campus open to everyone. We will always be with you so that your challenge becomes our pride.

Key Messages from the Vice Director

Customized Support for Student Success

We help learners design their desired paths and expand their achievement experiences through learning data analysis and regular coaching.

  • AI-based academic coaching and learning diagnosis service
  • Strengthening project-based classes and Capstone Design
  • Operation of alumni mentoring and career matching programs

Building a Sustainable Campus

We are creating a sustainable university model that grows with the local community by combining eco-friendly infrastructure and social contribution activities.

  • Digital campaigns to achieve carbon neutrality goals
  • Talent donation and volunteer programs linked to the region
  • Joint research projects with global partners

Building an International Campus

GTCC Cyber College is creating a university model that can educate all students around the world by creating regional learning centers worldwide.

  • 1st Phase: Korea Campus Construction
  • 2nd Phase: Nigeria Campus Construction
  • 3rd Phase: Vietnam Campus Construction
