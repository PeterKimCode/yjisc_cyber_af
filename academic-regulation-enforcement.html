Implementation Rules

Academic Regulation Enforcement Rules

Provides detailed procedures and standards for applying the basic principles set forth in the school regulations to actual academic operations. Clarifies the roles and responsibilities of each department to support stable academic operations.

Detailed Regulations

Schedule · Evaluation · Student Support

Section 1: Academic Administration

Regulates detailed procedures for establishing academic schedules, organizing classes, and administrative support.

  • The annual academic schedule is finalized by December of the previous year and announced to all members.
  • Course openings are confirmed after department deliberation and approval by the Office of Education Innovation.
  • Departments responsible for operating academic administration systems (LMS, Integrated Information System) are designated and management manuals are provided.
Dec Schedule Finalization

The academic schedule is confirmed and announced after a preliminary notice at least 2 weeks in advance.

Section 2: Class and Evaluation Management

Standardizes course operation and evaluation methods to ensure fair and transparent learning assessment.

  1. Syllabi serve review and approval procedures by submission 2 weeks before the semester starts.
  2. Evaluation methods must strictly specify composition ratios such as midterms, finals, assignments, and participation.
  3. Grade calculation and disclosure are completed within 14 days after the semester ends, and objection procedures are guided.

Quality Management Indicators

We check class quality by analyzing major indicators such as course satisfaction, learning participation rates, and grade distribution.

Check Course Operation Guidelines

Section 3: Academic Record Management

Details the criteria and procedures for processing academic record changes from admission to graduation.

  • Changes such as leave of absence, return, withdrawal, expulsion, and readmission are approved by the Academic Support Team after online application.
  • Applications for academic record changes must be submitted with digital signatures or scanned copies along with necessary supporting documents.
  • Results of academic record changes are notified via text and email within 3 business days.
3 Days Processing Time

Urgent reasons are processed with priority in consultation with the person in charge.

Section 4: Scholarship and Financial Support

Establishes detailed criteria for scholarship review, payment, and post-management to strengthen fairness and transparency.

Review Procedure

Application Receipt → Eligibility Review → Committee Deliberation → Result Announcement.

Payment Criteria

Evaluation items and weights by scholarship type, such as grades, income bracket, and activity performance, are disclosed in advance.

Post-Management

Scholarship recipients submit audit reports and participate in mentoring programs if necessary.

View Scholarship Rules

Section 5: Information Security and Privacy

Provides detailed guidelines to safely manage the personal information of students and faculty.

  • All systems apply 2-step verification, and access rights are checked periodically.
  • Personal information handlers must complete security training at least once a year.
  • In case of information leakage, report immediately and take action according to the response manual.

Security Reporting Center

Report immediately to security@gtcc.ac.kr in case of security incidents or suspicious cases.
